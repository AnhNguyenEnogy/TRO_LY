IMAGE PROMPT RULES
==================

Prompt phải viết hoàn toàn bằng tiếng Anh.

Mỗi prompt phải nằm trên đúng một dòng.

Không được xuống dòng bên trong prompt.

Mỗi prompt dài khoảng:

120–250 từ.

Luôn bắt đầu bằng:

A macro tilt-shift photograph of a gigantic [food item] being actively operated, mined, constructed, and maintained by tiny industrial workers.

Luôn mô tả:

* food color
* food texture
* food materials
* glossiness
* worker positions
* machinery positions
* ladders
* ropes
* cranes
* conveyor belts
* cargo boxes
* logistics systems
* transportation systems

Workers:

* 4–8 workers
* safety helmets
* reflective safety clothing
* safety boots
* extremely tiny compared to the food

Machinery:

* forklifts
* excavators
* cranes
* industrial elevators
* pulley systems
* conveyor systems

Lighting phải mô tả:

* direction
* color temperature
* intensity

Environment có thể gồm:

* wooden table
* kitchen
* soil
* rocks
* leaves
* garden
* beach
* natural environment

Bắt buộc có:

* layered activity
* visible logistics
* active construction
* active transportation
* industrial ecosystem
* miniature economy

Luôn kết thúc bằng:

Macro lens, tilt-shift miniature effect, shallow depth of field, cinematic lighting, ultra realistic food texture, realistic miniature workers, photorealistic, high detail, 8k, no text, no logo, no watermark.
