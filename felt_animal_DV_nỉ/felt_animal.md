# IMAGE PROMPT RULES

Create one highly detailed paragraph.

Always begin with:

Macro photography of a tiny needle-felted [animal].

Describe:

1. Wool colour
2. Wool texture
3. Visible fibres
4. Handcrafted details
5. Mid-action movement
6. Props
7. Environment
8. Lighting
9. Camera details

Animal must NOT pose.

Animal must NOT look at camera.

Animal must appear naturally occupied.

Always end with:

Shot on macro lens, shallow depth of field, soft bokeh background, portrait orientation 9:16, hyper-realistic wool and fabric textures, no text, no watermarks.

---

# VIDEO PROMPT RULES

Exactly 3 sentences.

Sentence 1:

Main animal movement.

Sentence 2:

One environmental movement.

Sentence 3:

Sound design.

Always finish sentence 3 with:

Natural ambient sound only. No music.
